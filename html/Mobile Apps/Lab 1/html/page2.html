How To Use The ICOCA Card

Now that you have your ICOCA card, you're going to want to know how to use it. This is very simple!

To use your card:

  • Walk to the gate where your train platform is
  • Touch your ICOCA card to the blue-rounded-square on the gate
  • The yellow flaps between the gate will open
  • walk through the gate and board your train

When you go to exit the train at your final station, you will follow the same steps mentioned above to exit the gates and leave the station.
